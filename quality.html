Quality & Infrastructure

An ISO 9001:2015 certified facility, disciplined quality control, and export-ready capabilities built over three decades.

How we ensure quality

Our Quality Process

Every product moves through the same disciplined checkpoints — from raw material to final packing.

01
Raw Material Inspection
Incoming brass, aluminium and steel are verified for grade and purity before they enter production.
02
Precision Machining
CNC and automatic machines hold tight tolerances, with in-process dimensional checks at every stage.
03
Finishing & Plating
Polishing, buffing and plating are controlled for consistent colour, coverage and corrosion resistance.
04
Final QC & Testing
Every batch is hand-inspected and function-tested against spec before it is approved for packing.
05
Export Packing
Protective, labelled packaging built for long-haul sea and air freight with full traceability.

Our facility

Manufacturing Infrastructure

Our Jamnagar facility combines high-precision CNC machining, automatic lathes, forging, casting and in-house plating lines under one roof. Vertical integration lets us control quality at every step and respond quickly to custom and OEM requirements.

  • CNC & automatic machining lines
  • In-house brass & aluminium casting
  • Polishing, buffing & plating units
  • Dedicated tool room for custom dies
  • Quality lab with measuring instruments
  • Export-grade packing & warehousing
