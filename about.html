Our story

From a Jamnagar Workshop to a Global Exporter

Abhay Brass was founded in 1995 in Jamnagar — the heart of India's brass manufacturing industry. What began as a modest workshop producing tower bolts for the local market has grown, over three decades, into a full-scale manufacturing operation exporting precision door hardware worldwide.

Our philosophy has never changed: make hardware that lasts. Every component is machined to tight tolerances, hand-inspected, and finished to resist a lifetime of daily use. That commitment has earned us the trust of architects, builders, OEMs and distributors across more than thirty countries.

Today, together with our sister manufacturing unit and export partner, we cover the complete hardware supply chain — from raw brass and aluminium casting to finished, export-ready fittings.
