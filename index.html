Who we are

Three Decades of Brass Craftsmanship

Founded in Jamnagar — India's brass city — in 1995, Abhay Brass has grown from a small workshop into a trusted manufacturer and global exporter of precision door hardware. Every tower bolt, hinge and latch we make is engineered to exacting tolerances and finished to last a lifetime.

Today we serve architects, builders, OEMs and hardware distributors in over 30 countries, backed by an ISO 9001:2015 certified facility and a team that treats every order — large or small — with the same care.
